Triangles... What makes them unique?

Different types of triangles exist. Depending on the length of each side, triangles can also have different names.
  • If your triangle has equal sides all-around, your triangle is an Equilateral triangle
  • If your triangle has only two equal sides, your triangle is an Isosceles triangle
  • If your triangle has no equal sides at all, your triangle is a Scalene triangle
